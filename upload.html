PhotoTrans - A Simple Photo-translation App

HOW IT WORKS

PhotoTrans extracts and translates text in images with the help of an OCR engine and Google's translation libraries.

Images uploaded are pre-processed before the OCR engine performs its magic on the image to extract text from it.

The extracted text is then translated to English for you to understand.


For your information: We do not store images uploaded to PhotoTrans, neither do we store any personal information about users such as location.
Your privacy is safe while using PhotoTrans. Happy Touring!

HOW TO USE PhotoTrans

Step 1: Click a picture with your smartphone camera. Place the text clearly in the image for best results.

Step 2: Upload picture to PhotoTrans.

Step 3: Translated text will be displayed on your screen.

It is that easy.
